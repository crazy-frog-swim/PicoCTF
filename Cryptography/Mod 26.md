## ROT 13
-	**unable** to **encrypt** the ***special character*** like **{ }_ ’**
-	**unable** to **encrypt** the ***number 0 – 9 ***
-	ciphertext and plaintext are the **same length **
-	**alphabet capital** in ciphertext is **same** in plaintext **A – Z**
